What is Colorveil

Music is the doorway. the world is what's behind it.

Colorveil Studios is a multimedia entertainment company creating original worlds inspired by music.

Every universe is a complete reality — sound, characters, lore, comics, animation, and interactive experiences, all woven into a single living mythology.
